In-Details

Interior Designer

Bhavika Goyal

Founder and creative head at ‘The Design Tale’ , Bhavika completed her Bachelors in Interior design from Dezyne Ecole College, Ajmer.

She is a national council member of WICCI – Interiors and Home Furnishing Council.

Before starting her own practice, Bhavika interned at Hiren Patel Architects, Ahmedabad and worked as Interior Designer at AJ Studios , Jaipur.

Bhavika has a creative personality, getting inspired from daily mundane things around her.She is an art enthusiasts, self motivated , loves exploring things and a keen learner.

About Me

How We Work !

We are one stop for creating diverse spaces in interior domain. We use an interactive design process which includes series of actions beginning with conceptual stage to the final execution, keeping client’s requirements in harmony.

We make progress in many stages

Programming

This is the phase where we sit and talk about what you have to create, the brief of the project.

Conceptualising

We will come back to you with the game plan, complete with sketches, swatches, presentationds and all that good stuff so you can get an idea of what we have in mind for your space.

Procurrment and delivery

We will deliver the complete set of drawings and details.

Project management

The painting, wallpapering, flooring installation and the likes happens during this phase. we will keep everything on track and manage any necessary subcontractors.
